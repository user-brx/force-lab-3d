Teacher's Guide

Force Lab 3D - support material for Physics classes (high school and intro to college).

Every scene in the simulator demonstrates a principle of mechanics with real physics - no made-up numbers. The pages below explain, at a classroom level, where each formula comes from, which real values the program uses, and include a worked example and book references.

Suggested use: project the simulator, change one parameter at a time, and ask students to predict the result before dropping/firing. Then compare the prediction with the HUD (speed, forces, energy). The formulas here let you check the numbers by hand.
