Numerical validations (examples)

  • Tsiolkovsky: the simulated vacuum speed matches Δv = Isp·g₀·ln(m₀/mf) within < 3 %.
  • Energy conservation: car (motor = kinetic + dissipated), skaters (push = kinetic), airplane (motor = kinetic + potential + drag) - all within integration error.
  • .50 BMG: muzzle energy ~16.6 kJ; steel penetration ~25-35 mm (real ~25-30 mm); a horizontal shot reaches the ground within a few hundred meters (½gt²).
  • Impact/meteor: 500 t at 15 km/s ≈ 13 kton of TNT, ~280 m crater - Collins (2005) and Glasstone & Dolan scaling.

Known simplifications (honest, not inventions)

  • Semi-implicit Euler integration (step ≤ 1/240 s): ~1-3 % error vs. analytic.
  • 3rd law: the planet's acceleration is computed and shown, but the planet is not actually displaced (huge mass → invisible).
  • Constant rolling resistance; conservative sea-level Isp (260 s); single-lapse ISA stratosphere.
  • No meteor ablation/fragmentation on reentry (the drag deceleration is real).
Verdict: the physics is real and validated by tests. Nothing was made up - every number has a source.
